About Me

Hello, My name is Brandon Lee. I am currently working as an AI research lead at roboeye.ai. Our mission is to introduce cutting-edge algorithms in CV and AI to industry automation.

Even though I am spending most of my times in CV nowadays, my background is in NLP, specifically speech recognition. I was a Master's student in computer science at the University of Waterloo, publishing under the guidance of Jimmy Lin. I have spent many years in developing fully productionized yet open-source wake word detection toolkit with a web browser deployment target, Howl. Luckily, my effort has been picked up by Mozilla's Firefox Voice and it is actively providing a completely hands-free experience to many users all over the world.

I am not tied to a particular domain. I am eager to learn various impacts DL makes in our daily lives. Having said that, I have hands-on experience in GAN and multi-task learning as well.
