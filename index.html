Hello, I'm Brandon Lee.

I'm currently working as an AI research lead at RoboEye.ai integrating cutting-edge algorithms in computer vision (CV) and AI into industrial automation solutions.

Though I am spending most of my time in CV nowadays, my background is in natural language processing (NLP), specifically speech recognition. I was a Master's student in computer science at the University of Waterloo, publishing under the guidance of Jimmy Lin. I have spent many years developing a fully productionized yet open-source wake word detection toolkit with a web browser deployment target, Howl. Fortunately, my effort has been picked up by Mozilla's Firefox Voice and it is actively providing a completely hands-free experience to many users all over the world.

I have recently published a book titled Production-Ready Applied Deep Learning! The book explains how to convert a deep learning model into a production-ready application optimized for a particular production setting.
