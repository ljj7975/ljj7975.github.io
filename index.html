Hello, I'm Brandon Lee.


As a research scientist at Epson's Computer Vision & Robotics Lab, I am currently developing a 2D vision system that utilizes language to communicate effectively with users and adapt efficiently to dynamic environments.

Although my recent work has focused on computer vision and robotics, I completed a master's degree under the guidance of Jimmy Lin with a focus on natural language processing (NLP), specifically speech recognition. I developed Howl, a JavaScript-based keyword spotting system integrated into Mozilla's Firefox Voice to provide a hands-free experience for users worldwide.

I have also authored a book titled Production-Ready Applied Deep Learning, which explains how to convert a deep learning model into a production-ready application optimized for specific production settings.
